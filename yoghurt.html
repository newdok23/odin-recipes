Description

Here's a classic fudgy brownie recipe — rich, chocolatey, with a crackly top and chewy center.

Ingredients

  • 170g (6 oz / ¾ cup) unsalted butter
  • 200g (1 cup) granulated sugar
  • 100g (½ cup, packed) light brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 100g (¾ cup) all-purpose flour
  • 50g (½ cup) unsweetened cocoa powder (use Dutch-processed for deeper flavor)
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 100g (3.5 oz / ⅔ cup) semi-sweet or dark chocolate chips or chunks (optional but recommended)

Steps

  1. Preheat & Prep
    • Preheat your oven to 175°C (350°F).
    • Line an 8x8 inch (20x20 cm) square pan with parchment paper and lightly grease.
  2. Melt Butter & Sugars
    • In a saucepan or microwave, melt the butter.
    • Stir in granulated and brown sugars while warm — this helps create that shiny, crackly top.
  3. Add Eggs & Vanilla
    • Let the mixture cool slightly, then whisk in eggs one at a time.
    • Stir in vanilla extract.
  4. Mix Dry Ingredients
    • In a separate bowl, sift together flour, cocoa powder, and salt.
    • Fold dry ingredients into the wet mixture until just combined (don’t overmix).
      • >
  5. Add Chocolate
    • Fold in chocolate chips or chunks if using.
  6. Bake
  7. Pour butter in a prepared pan and smooth the top
  8. Bake for 25–30 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with a few moist crumbs (not wet batter).
  9. Cool & Serve
    • Cool in the pan for at least 15 minutes before slicing.
    • For clean cuts, chill the brownies first and use a sharp knife.

Optional Addons

  • Nuts: Add chopped walnuts or pecans (about ½ cup)
  • Espresso: Add 1 tsp espresso powder to enhance the chocolate flavor
  • Salted caramel drizzle on top before baking for extra decadence
