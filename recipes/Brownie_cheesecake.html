Brownie Cheesecake

Brownie_cheesecake

Description

This easy Cheesecake Brownies recipe is the best of both worlds - fudgy brownie base topped with a brownie cheesecake topping.

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter
  • Dark chocolate
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Plain Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Salt

    • Cooking process

    1. Make brownie butter and save it for later
    2. Bake this then let it cool
    3. Make cheesecake butter
    4. Add batter on top of brownie
    5. Swirl the extra brownie batter on top
    6. Bake it in a water bath
    7. Let the cheesecake cool in the oven for an hour
    8. Take the cheesecake out of the oven and let it cool overnight
    9. Enjoy
