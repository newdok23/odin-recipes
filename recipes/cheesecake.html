Description

To cook this delicious looking cheesecake you will need a number of easily obtainable ingredients and around 40 minutes of preparation time plus 20 minutes of cooking time .

Ingredients

Crust:

  • 200g (about 2 cups) graham cracker crumbs (or digestive biscuits)
  • 100g (7 tbsp) unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar (optional)

Filling:

  • 900g (2 lbs) full-fat cream cheese, softened
  • 200g (1 cup) granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp lemon zest (optional but adds a nice tang)
  • 4 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 240ml (1 cup) sour cream (room temperature)

Steps

  1. Prep the Oven and Pan
    • Preheat Oven to 160 C
    • Line the bottom of a 9-inch (23 cm) springform pan with parchment paper and grease the sides.
  2. Make the Crust
    • Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar.
    • Press into the bottom of the pan firmly and slightly up the sides.
    • Bake for 10 minutes, then let it cool while you make the filling.
  3. Make the Cheesecake filling
    • Beat the cream cheese until smooth (no lumps!).
    • Add sugar, flour, vanilla, and lemon zest; mix until fully combined.
    • Add eggs one at a time, beating on low speed.
    • Add sour cream and mix just until smooth.
  4. Bake the Cheesecake
    • Pour the filling into the crust.
    • Water bath method (recommended): Wrap the outside of the springform pan with aluminum foil and place it in a larger pan filled with 1–2 inches of hot water.
    • Bake for 55–65 minutes, until edges are set but the center still slightly jiggles.
  5. Cool Slowly
    • Turn off oven, crack the door open, and let the cheesecake sit for 1 hour.
    • Then remove from the oven and cool at room temperature for another hour.
    • Chill in the fridge for at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.

Optional Toppings:

  • Fresh Berries
  • Fruid Compote
  • Caramel or chocolate sauce
  • Whipped cream
